Split

Split is an AI-powered expense tracking bot that helps groups track who owes who via SMS.

How it works

Users opt in by texting START to the Split number. The bot then allows users to log shared expenses, debts, and settlements via natural language messages. Users can opt out at any time by texting STOP.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

What we collect

  • Your phone number (to send and receive messages)
  • Expense messages you send to the bot
  • Balance data (who owes who, amounts)

What we don't collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not share your data with anyone outside your expense group
  • We do not collect payment information

SMS messaging

By texting START to our number, you consent to receive SMS messages from Split. Message frequency varies based on group activity. Message and data rates may apply. Text STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Text HELP for help.

Data retention

Your expense data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion by texting DELETE to the bot.
